Sheep Meat

As a premium supplier of high-quality lamb and mutton, our offerings include fresh, native organic pasture-raised Merino and Dorper breeds, as well as grain-fed lamb aged 60–90 days. Our products meet the highest global standards, comparable to the finest New Zealand exports, ensuring superior taste and texture.

  • Grass-Fed – Native Merino & Dorper breeds
  • Grain-Fed - 60–90 days for premium marbling

Our strict quality control and ethical sourcing practices guarantee top-tier meat products tailored for international markets. Partnering with us ensures consistent, high-grade sheep meat that meets global culinary and regulatory standards.

Beef

We offer premium beef selections, from tender veal to high-grade grain-fed cuts, ensuring superior taste and quality.

  • Bob Veal – 30-day milk-fed calf
  • White Veal – 90-day milk-fed calf
  • Pink Veal – Mixed milk & hay-fed (1–90 days)
  • Yearling – 60–90 days grain-fed steers
  • Grass-Fed Beef – 24–30 months
  • Premium Beef – 180-day grain-fed, equivalent to US Prime Plus

Our rigorous quality control and ethical sourcing ensure top-tier beef, meeting global culinary and regulatory standards.

Goat Meat

    Our goat meat selection is carefully sourced to ensure superior quality, tenderness, and rich flavor. From delicate milk-fed baby goats to premium organic pasture-raised options, our products meet the highest global standards for excellence.

  • Goat (Cabrito) – 30–45 days, milk-fed
  • Kid Goat – 6–9 months, virgin organic pasture grass-fed
  • Goat Mutton – 12–24 months, virgin organic pasture grass-fed

With a focus on ethical sourcing and rigorous quality control, we provide premium goat meat tailored for global markets. Partnering with us guarantees consistent, high-quality cuts that align with culinary and regulatory standards worldwide.

Leather

Leather tanneries are key partners to Bilays International Exporters, providing:

  • Full-Grain Wet Blue Leather
  • Full-Grain Wet Blue Leather
  • Full-Grain Wet Blue Leather

We export premium wet blue cow leather to global markets, serving industries such as fashion, upholstery, and automotive manufacturing with exceptional quality and reliability.
