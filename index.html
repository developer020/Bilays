KenMeat

As the largest slaughterhouse and meat processing facility in Eastern and Central Africa, KenMeat operates under the Export Processing Authority, ensuring adherence to international standards such as:

  • HACCP certifications
  • ISO certifications

Their state-of-the-art facilities and rigorous quality control processes guarantee premium meat products that meet the stringent requirements of global markets. Partnering with KenMeat allows Bilays International to confidently deliver high-quality, ethically sourced, and safely processed meat to its clients worldwide.

Neema Investment Limited

Neema Livestock and Slaughtering Investment Limited is a valued partner. Specializing in:

  • Livestock Rearing
  • Halal Slaughtering
  • Modern & Ethical Slaughtering Practices

Neema ensures the highest standards of hygiene, animal welfare, and food safety. Their operations are designed to meet international export requirements, including compliance with global certifications. By partnering with Neema Livestock and Slaughtering Investment Limited, Bilays International gains access to premium, ethically sourced meat products, enabling the delivery of superior quality goods to clients worldwide.

Juja International Abattoir

    Juja International Abattoir is a strategic partner to Bilays International Exporters, specializing in:

  • Large-Scale Slaughtering
  • Initial Meat Processing

Juja International Abattoir bridges the gap by providing bulk slaughtering services that meet rigorous hygiene and animal welfare standards. This partnership ensures a seamless supply chain, enabling Bilays International to deliver consistent, high-quality meat products to global markets, while maintaining efficiency and scalability across all stages of production.
